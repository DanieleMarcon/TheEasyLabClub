# 09-accessibility-gdpr.md — The EasyLab Club

## 1. Purpose

Define rules for:

* Accessibility (WCAG compliance)
* GDPR (data protection)

These rules ensure:

* usability for all users
* legal compliance
* system scalability

---

## 2. Accessibility Principles

The site must be:

* perceivable
* operable
* understandable
* robust

---

## 3. Semantic Structure

Use proper HTML structure:

* correct heading hierarchy
* semantic tags (header, main, section, footer)
* no layout-only div abuse

---

## 4. Text Readability

* sufficient font size
* high contrast
* clear typography

Avoid:

* low contrast text
* overly small fonts

---

## 5. Navigation Accessibility

* keyboard navigable
* visible focus states
* logical tab order

---

## 6. Buttons & Links

* must be clearly distinguishable
* must have descriptive labels

Avoid:

* “click here”
* unclear actions

---

## 7. Images

* all images must have alt text
* alt must describe meaning, not decoration

---

## 8. Forms Accessibility

* labels must be explicit
* error messages must be clear
* no hidden required fields

---

## 9. Motion & Effects

* avoid excessive animation
* respect reduced-motion preferences

---

## 10. GDPR Principles

The system must follow:

* data minimization
* purpose limitation
* transparency

---

## 11. Data Collection Rules

Only collect:

* name
* email
* company (optional)

Avoid:

* unnecessary data
* sensitive data

---

## 12. Consent

Before collecting data:

* user must give explicit consent
* consent must be clear and informed

---

## 13. Cookies

Must include:

* cookie banner
* ability to accept/reject
* no tracking before consent

---

## 14. Privacy Policy

Must clearly state:

* what data is collected
* why it is collected
* how it is used
* how long it is stored

---

## 15. Data Storage (Future)

* secure storage
* limited access
* no unnecessary retention

---

## 16. Forms (Critical)

All forms must:

* explain purpose
* include consent checkbox
* not auto-subscribe

---

## 17. Email Handling

* no automatic marketing opt-in
* explicit opt-in required

---

## 18. AI & Data (Important)

If AI is used:

* do not send personal data without consent
* anonymize when possible

---

## 19. Legal Pages (Required)

* Privacy Policy
* Cookie Policy
* Terms (optional for V1, recommended)

---

## 20. Critical Rule

If a feature requires data:

→ justify it
→ minimize it
→ make it transparent

No hidden data usage allowed.
