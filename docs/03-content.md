# 03-content.md --- The EasyLab Club

## Offer Structure

The EasyLab Club is structured into 4 levels:

-   Level 0 → Free (entry point)
-   Level 1 → Lab (premium modules)
-   Level 2 → Programs (team transformation)
-   Level 3 → Network (continuity)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Programs (for Teams)

### AI & Workflow for Teams

Reduce operational time and increase efficiency using AI and automation.

### Tools & Digital Productivity

### Organization & Processes

## Network

The Network is the continuity layer of the system.

It is not an open community.

Core idea: "Learning does not stop after a module."
