# 00-project-overview.md --- The EasyLab Club

## What it is

The EasyLab Club is an operational learning system designed to improve
how people and teams work.

## Goal

Turn learning into real-world application and measurable improvement.

## What it is NOT

-   generic e-learning platform
-   open community
-   course marketplace

## What it IS

-   structured environment
-   modular system
-   continuous improvement model

## Target

-   teams and companies
-   professionals
-   individuals focused on improving how they work

## Ecosystem Role

-   Lavoro Sporco → diagnosis and direction
-   EasyLab Club → operational learning
-   Idola → AI & automation
-   Meraviglia → marketing
